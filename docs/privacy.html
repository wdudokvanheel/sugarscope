Privacy Policy

Version 1.0

Scope

This privacy policy applies to the GlucoScope iOS app.

Data Handling

GlucoScope visualizes blood glucose data by connecting directly to a server chosen by you. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health data. All data remains on your device and/or the server you select.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or any third‑party tracking services. Your privacy is fully respected and protected.

Open Source

The full source code of GlucoScope is available as open source on GitHub.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to open an issue on the GlucoScope GitHub repository.
